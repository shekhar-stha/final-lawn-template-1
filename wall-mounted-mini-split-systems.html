Deluxe Wall-Mounted Indoor Unit

The MSZ-FS Deluxe Wall-mounted Indoor Unit features a 3D i-see Sensor® and offers dual vane oper ation. The 3D i-see Sensor scans the room, measuring temperature and occupant location. Indirect or Direct airflow settings divert supply air away from or toward room occupants. The unit's interior air duct/vane, coil, and fan features Dual Barrier Coating, which maintains efficiency by keeping the inside clean.
